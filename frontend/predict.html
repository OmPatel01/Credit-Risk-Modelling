📊 Prediction Results

How to read these results:
• Probability of Default (PD): Estimated likelihood the borrower will miss payments
• Credit Score (Scorecard only): Interpretable score from 576–906; higher = lower risk
• Risk Level: Low (<5% PD) · Medium (5–20%) · High (>20%)
• Decision: Approve (Low) · Review (Medium) · Reject (High)
